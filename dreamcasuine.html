Services We Offer

Our services range from our small service catering events to premium large party events.
We want you to choose the service that's right for and have the best expirence possible.



Basic (Small Party Event)

This is our basic service containg meals for 25 to 50 meals. Excellent for a small lunch in, or backyard pool party.


Mid (Medium Sized Event)

Our Mid range catering service offers meals for 75 to 150 person's. This service is perfect for a seminar or confrence like setting.


Premium (Large Scale Event)

Our premium service offers meals for over 200 to 300 person's or requested amount. This service is excellent for weddings and other large party events.


Our services our based on a tier level arrangment.This depends on horderves,
