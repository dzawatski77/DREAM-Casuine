Services We Offer

Whether you're planning a small gathering with close friends or a large corporate event, we've got you covered. Our team is dedicated to providing a personalized and enjoyable experience for all of our clients, no matter the size of the event. Let us take care of the food, so you can focus on making memories with your guests.



Basic (Small Party Event)

Looking for a catering option that covers the essentials for your event? Look no further than our basic package! Our basic package is designed to provide delicious meals for events with 25 to 50 guests. Let us take care of the food while you enjoy your special occasion.


Mid (Medium Sized Event)

Indulge in our Mid catering package for your event with 75 to 150 guests. Our Mid package offers an exceptional culinary experience, featuring the finest ingredients and expertly crafted dishes. From hors d'oeuvres to desserts, our team is dedicated to providing an unforgettable dining experience for your guests.


Premium (Large Scale Event)

Planning a grand event for 200 to 300 guests? Our premium catering package is perfect for you! Our premium package is designed to impress, featuring a wide range of delectable dishes crafted with the freshest ingredients. Our experienced team will work closely with you to create a customized menu that suits your tastes and preferences. Trust us to make your event a memorable one.


Our services our based on a tier level arrangment.This depends on hors d'oeuvres,
appitizers, dessets and food items. We also offer B.Y.O.(Build Your Own) catering options.
